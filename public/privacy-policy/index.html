Privacy Policy

Icon Produce Internal CRM Application · Effective June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Icon Produce LLC ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information within the Icon Produce internal CRM application (the "App"). The App is an internal tool used exclusively by Icon Produce employees.

1. Information we collect

The App collects the following categories of information:

  • Account information: employee name, work email, phone number, and assigned role (sales representative, data entry or manager).
  • Customer information: business names, contact names, phone numbers, email addresses, purchase-day preferences, and free-form notes about the customer, entered by employees.
  • Activity data: sales interactions logged by employees (calls, texts, emails, outcomes such as "ordered", "no answer", "callback", timestamps).
  • SMS delivery data: phone numbers of employees who receive SMS reminders and the delivery status of each message.
We do not collect or process: financial account numbers, government IDs, health data, or precise geolocation.

2. How we use information

Information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Operating the App and its features (customer tracking, activity logging, analytics, reports).
  • Sending internal workflow SMS reminders to employees who have provided consent (see our SMS Consent page for details).
  • Improving the App based on usage patterns.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is shared only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database hosting), Vercel (application hosting), Twilio (SMS delivery). These providers act as data processors under strict contracts.
  • Legal compliance: when required by law, court order, or valid legal process.

4. SMS messaging

Phone numbers used for SMS reminders are used only to deliver those reminders and are not shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Employees can opt out of SMS reminders at any time by replying STOP. Details are in our SMS Consent page.

5. Data security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect information: encrypted database connections, role-based access control (only the manager sees company-wide data; sales representatives see only their own customers), and secure authentication through Supabase Auth.

6. Data retention

Customer and interaction data is retained while the employee is active and for up to 24 months after employment ends, then archived or deleted. SMS delivery logs are retained for 12 months for operational and compliance purposes.

7. Your rights

Employees have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about them.
  • Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Request deletion of their personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for SMS reminders at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact the sales manager or use the contact information below.

8. Children

The App is not intended for and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to employees directly. The "Effective" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent version.

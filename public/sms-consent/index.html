SMS Reminder Consent

Internal CRM workflow notifications for sales representatives

What are these messages?

Icon Produce LLC sends SMS reminders to our sales representatives (employees) as part of our internal workflow. These messages contain a daily summary of pending customer accounts that the rep has not yet contacted, along with optional notes from the sales manager.

Who receives these messages?
Only Icon Produce LLC employees who have provided their personal cell phone number during onboarding and explicitly consented to receive workflow reminders.

How do employees give consent?

Employees provide their personal cell phone number to the sales manager during onboarding at Icon Produce LLC (7850 NW 25th St, Doral, FL 33122). Written consent is captured on the employee onboarding form, which is retained in HR records. By signing the form and providing their number for this specific purpose, employees consent to receive workflow reminder SMS from Icon Produce LLC.

Sample message

"Icon Produce reminder: You have 8 pending customers today. Top 3: Mary's Bistro, Joe's Market, Sunrise Foods. Note from Jorge: don't forget to follow up on price lists. Reply STOP to opt out."

Message frequency

Employees may receive up to 3 SMS reminders per day, on scheduled workdays only. Actual frequency depends on the manager's reminder settings in the internal CRM.

Rates and charges

Standard message and data rates may apply. Icon Produce LLC does not charge for these messages, but your mobile carrier's rates apply based on your plan.

How to opt out

Employees can opt out of receiving these messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message received
  • Replying UNSUBSCRIBE or CANCEL
  • Contacting the sales manager directly at the number below

Upon opt-out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further workflow reminders will be sent to your number.

Help

For help or questions about these messages, reply HELP to any message or contact us using the information below.

Privacy

Phone numbers collected for SMS reminders are used exclusively for this internal workflow purpose. They are not shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing. For our full privacy practices, see our Privacy Policy.
